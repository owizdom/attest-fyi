How it works

Three checks, one seal

I

Fingerprint

Challenge prompts engineered so a swapped, distilled, or quantised engine gives itself away — log-probs, canaries, refusal boundaries.

II

Audit

Verify the TDX / SGX / enclave quote and image digest, then weigh it against what actually came out. A valid seal on the wrong engine is the headline fault.

III

Reproduce

Every probe set and raw transcript is public and content-hashed. Re-run the harness yourself and you land on the same verdict. Blind spots are published, not hidden.
